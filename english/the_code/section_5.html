Section 5

You must act with integrity and uphold public trust and confidence in the social care profession.

In particular you must not:

  • 1. Directly or indirectly abuse, neglect or harm individuals, carers or colleagues
  • 2. Exploit individuals, carers or colleagues in any way
  • 3. Abuse the trust of individuals and carers or the access you have to personal information about them, or to their property, home or workplace
  • 4. Form inappropriate personal relationships with individuals, their families or carers
  • 5. Discriminate unlawfully or unjustifiably against individuals, carers, colleagues or other people
  • 6. Condone any unlawful or unjustifiable discrimination by individuals, carers, colleagues or other people
  • 7. Put yourself or other people at unnecessary risk
  • 8. Behave in a way, in work or outside work, which would call into question your suitability to work in the social care profession
