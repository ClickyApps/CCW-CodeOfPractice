Section 7

In addition to sections 1 â€“ 6, if you are responsible for managing or leading staff, you must embed the Code in their work.

This includes:

  • 1. Ensuring staff know about the Code and how it applies to their conduct and practice
  • 2. Supporting an open and learning culture in the workplace where staff have the opportunity to raise concerns and access, discuss, reflect on and share best practice
  • 3. Ensuring induction, training, learning and development opportunities support staff to maintain and develop their knowledge, skills and understanding
  • 4. Providing supervision and appraisal to guide, support and motivate staff to meet their role, responsibilities and accountabilities
  • 5. Taking steps to know what is happening in the delivery of social care and support provided by your staff and giving constructive feedback
  • 6. Monitoring performance and taking immediate steps to address unsatisfactory performance or misconduct by staff
