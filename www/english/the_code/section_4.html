Section 4

You must respect the rights of individuals while seeking to ensure that their behaviour does not harm themselves or other people.

This includes:

  • 1. Working with individuals to balance rights, responsibilities and risks
  • 2. Following risk assessment policies and procedures to assess whether the behaviour of individuals presents a risk of harm to themselves or other people
  • 3. Taking necessary steps to minimise the risks of individualsâ€™ behaviour causing actual or potential harm to themselves or other people
  • 4. Ensuring that relevant colleagues and agencies are informed about the outcomes and implications of risk assessments
