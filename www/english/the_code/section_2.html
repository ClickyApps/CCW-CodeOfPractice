Section 2

You must strive to establish and maintain the trust and confidence of individuals and carers.

This includes:

  • 1. Being honest and trustworthy
  • 2. Communicating in an appropriate, open, accurate and straightforward way
  • 3. Actively supporting individuals and carers to communicate their views and preferences using their preferred method and language
  • 4. Respecting confidential information and clearly explaining policies about confidentiality to individuals and carers
  • 5. Being reliable and dependable
  • 6. Honouring work commitments, agreements and arrangements and, when it is not possible to do so, explaining why to individuals and carers
  • 7. Declaring issues that might create conflicts of interest and taking steps to ensure that they do not influence your professional judgement or practice
  • 8. Adhering to policies and procedures about accepting gifts and money from individuals, their families and carers
